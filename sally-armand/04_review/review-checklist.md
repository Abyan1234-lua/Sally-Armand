# Review Checklist

Sally runs every persona through this checklist before output.

---

## Section Completeness

- [ ] Simulation Header is present and fully filled
- [ ] All core demographic fields populated (name, age, job, location, income)
- [ ] Psychographic section has at least 3 specific details (not categories)
- [ ] Goals section has at least 2 goals — one professional, one personal
- [ ] Pain points are specific to this person's life, not generic market pain
- [ ] Buying behavior section describes a real decision process with a real past example
- [ ] Objections section has at least 2 objections, with the real reason behind each
- [ ] Pitch guide section contains at least one "what NOT to say" rule
- [ ] Quote section contains 2–3 things this person would actually say out loud

---

## Quality Checks

- [ ] No field contains the word "various", "multiple", "several", or "different" as a substitute for specifics
- [ ] No two personas in the batch share a job title
- [ ] The persona has at least one internal contradiction or tension
- [ ] The persona's objections are rooted in their biography, not just generic risk aversion
- [ ] The simulation header correctly reflects the persona's brief-fit score and response style
- [ ] The persona's voice in the quote section sounds distinct from other personas in the batch

---

## Simulation Readiness

- [ ] If you loaded this file into a Claude Project and said "I want to pitch my product to you" — would this persona respond in character?
- [ ] Does the simulation header tell Claude exactly how to behave?
- [ ] Is the brief-fit score assigned and does it match the persona's profile?

---

## Failure Protocol

**1–2 items failed:** Fix inline, note in `review-summary.md`
**3+ items failed:** Rewrite the affected sections from scratch
**Simulation header missing:** Always a blocker — must be added before output
